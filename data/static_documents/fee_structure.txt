Fee Structure for Undergraduate Programs (2025-2026)

The following is the annual fee structure for each branch under the B.Tech program:

1. Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
   - Tuition Fee: ₹1,25,000
   - Development Fee: ₹15,000
   - Exam & Lab Fee: ₹10,000
   - Total: ₹1,50,000 per annum

2. Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
   - Tuition Fee: ₹1,10,000
   - Development Fee: ₹15,000
   - Exam & Lab Fee: ₹10,000
   - Total: ₹1,35,000 per annum

3. Information Technology (IT)
   - Tuition Fee: ₹1,20,000
   - Development Fee: ₹15,000
   - Exam & Lab Fee: ₹10,000
   - Total: ₹1,45,000 per annum

Other Charges:
- Hostel (Optional): ₹60,000 per annum (excluding mess charges)
- Mess Charges: ₹36,000 per annum

Fee Payment Deadline:
- Full annual fee must be paid within 10 days of receiving the admission confirmation email.
- Late fee of ₹1,000 will be applicable per week after the deadline.

Scholarship:
- Scholarships available for students with 90% and above in PCM.
- Loan facilities are available through partner banks upon eligibility.
